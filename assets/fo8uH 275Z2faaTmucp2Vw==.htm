Infineon Technologies AG is a world leader in semiconductor solutions that make life easier, safer and greener. Microelectronics from Infineon is the key to a better future. In the 2019 fiscal year (ending 30 September), the company reported sales of around €8 billion with about 41,400 employees worldwide. Job Profile

Description of Internship: 
** Internship for duration of 11 Months effective from  1st July 2023 till 31st May 2024. Identified & selected students will be working on live projects of R&D depts. Like Automotive, Post Silicon validation, Connected Secure System, Power Sensor System, Design Enabling Services and  IT focusing on Embedded Systems and Securities, VLSI,  ADAS, AUTOSAR, Low level Device Drivers, Bluetooth, Wifi, Analog, Digital design and verification, USB, Sensors, Power electronics, Java card, System C, etc. detailed overview will be given later.

Leave: B.Tech Students:
** 16 Days annual internship leave and 11 Days sick leave.
